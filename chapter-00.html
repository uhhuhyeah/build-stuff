This is the part where you download Google Chrome.

Step 1 - Download Google Chrome.

Go to the Google Chrome download page and click on the big button that says “Download Google Chrome”.

Follow the instructions for installing Google Chrome to your computer.

Launch Google Chrome.

You already have Google Chrome! You're awesome! Please proceed to Step 2.

Step 2 - Open the Console.

Google Chrome has this awesome thing called the Console that can be used to interact with all kinds of stuff. It's a fully-capable tool that professional developers regularly use to develop, test, and debug.

To open the Console, click on the “wrench” icon in the top corner of Google Chrome.

Now hover over “Tools” and select “JavaScript Console”.

You should see the Console pop open either as a separate window or as a little sub-window within your main browser window.

If you're a real fancy pants, you may have noticed there is a keyboard shortcut available for opening the Console. Use it if you want to. Now your pants are even more fancy.

Step 3 - Make sure you know how to open Google Chrome and open the console.

Seriously, you need to make sure you know how to open Google Chrome. With any luck, part of the installation process placed a launcher somewhere that's easy to see.

This is not a joke. Close your current Google Chrome browser session and make sure you know how to get it back open. Then make sure you know how to open the Console.
